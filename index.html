    20-20-20 RULE

    The 20-20-20 rule helps reduce eye strain from screen use: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This simple practice relaxes eye muscles and prevents symptoms like headaches and blurred vision, promoting better eye health during extended screen time.

    Eye Care Reminder

    It's important to take regular breaks to rest your eyes.
